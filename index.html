About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an intensive 8-week live cohort program designed to help professionals, researchers, and students build practical AI solutions that supercharge productivity. Delivered by GRAPH Courses, a nonprofit initiative based at the University of Geneva, the course blends academic rigor with hands-on learning. Participants are guided through weekly live workshops, help sessions, and forums to build confidence applying AI in their daily workflows. This course is ideal for researchers, analysts, educators, and professionals seeking to integrate AI into their daily work — without needing to code. For more information, visit the official course page.

    • Develop custom chatbots to support projects and teams
