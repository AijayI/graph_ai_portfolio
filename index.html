About Me

I’m Ijeoma, a Senior Business Advisor at Vancouver Coastal Health with a decade of experience transforming complex healthcare data into actionable insights. Armed with an MPH from the University of Manchester, UK, and certifications in Advanced Epidemiological Data Science and Biostatistics, I bridge the worlds of public health, analytics, and organizational change. My current passion is harnessing Generative AI to streamline hospital forecasting and empower teams to make data‑driven decisions faster.

  • Core Skills: SQL • Python • R • Data Visualization • Forecasting • Generative AI
  • What I'm doing now: Automating data pipelines, building AI‑powered dashboards, and exploring LLM agents for rapid literature reviews and analyzing unstructured text data.
  • Outside work: Soccer mom to four energetic kids and an avid lover of coastal walks.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an eight‑week intensive where participants build practical AI solutions that supercharge productivity. Over the next two months, I will:

  • Automate time‑consuming document, spreadsheet, and data tasks
  • Use AI tools for knowledge organization and literature reviews
  • Develop custom chatbots to support projects and teams
  • Create professional visuals and presentations with AI
  • Apply these skills in a hands‑on capstone project
  • Receive real‑time support in live workshops, help sessions & online forums
